Today I tell you about WH questions

  • who? for people
  • why? for reasons
  • where? Places
  • What? In general
  • when? dates
  • wich? as what, but with limits (options)
  • whose? for belongings
  • how? in spanish cómo

Structure: WH + Auxiliar Verb + Subject/pronoun + verb + complement.

open questions with WH (information questions),exapmle: When did You visit my apartment?

close questions without WH,example: did You make a cake last night? yes, I did.

examples made by me

  • in present we have:
  • do/does
  • What do you like?
  • am/is/are
  • What is your favorite color?
  • in past we have:
  • did
  • did you eat seafood yesterday?
  • Was/Were
  • Which was your favorite question beteween this options?
